Mind Bunny

About this game

Welcome to Mind Bunny, a fast-paced, colorful game designed to enhance your speed, focus, and quick thinking!
Bunnies in various colors and sizes appear on the screen, and your task is to tap them according to the instructions provided by the game. Sounds easy? Think again! A rapidly dropping time bar makes every second a challenge, turning this simple concept into an exciting reaction game suitable for all ages.
Mind Bunny combines color matching, memory exercises, and quick tapping gameplay into one dynamic experience that players of different ages will enjoy.
